THE CLASSIC EXPERIENCE

Craftsmanship meets tradition

Cervus Barbershop brings the art of traditional barbering to Sector 2, Bucharest. Located at the Carol I / Calea Mosilor intersection, we've been serving the community since 2022 with precision cuts and authentic grooming services.

Our old school approach means attention to detail, quality products, and a relaxed atmosphere where you can unwind. Every service includes styling and personal consultation.
